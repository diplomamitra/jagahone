# JagahOne Dashboard UI Design System

## Overview

Design a modern SaaS dashboard UI for a platform called **JagahOne**.

The product focuses on:
- Google Business Profile management
- Local SEO tools
- AI review replies
- Rank tracking
- Audits & reports
- Competitor tracking
- Billing & subscription management
- Social media profile links
- QR review systems

The interface must feel:
- Clean
- Enterprise-level
- Minimal
- Modern
- Premium
- Fast & lightweight
- Highly usable

Avoid dark themes.
Avoid neon cyberpunk styles.
Avoid glassmorphism overload.
Avoid gaming UI aesthetics.

# Layout Structure

## Dashboard Layout

- Main content area
- Large rounded cards
- Spacious padding

### Sidebar Style
- White background
- Thin borders

### Top Header
Include:
- Date filter

---

# Card Design Language

## Card Style

Use:
- Rounded corners (24px–32px)
- Very soft shadows
- Light borders
- White background
- Clean spacing

Avoid:
- Hard borders
- Strong gradients
- Heavy shadows
- Sharp corners

## Metrics Cards

Include:
- Small circular icon background
- Large metric number
- Small growth percentage
- Tiny graph line

# Final Design Direction

The final product should look like:
- A premium local SEO SaaS platform
- Similar quality level to:
  - Stripe Dashboard
  - Linear
  - Notion
  - HubSpot
  - SEMrush
  - Framer SaaS dashboards

But customized for:
- Google Business Profile
- Local SEO
- AI business automation
- Small business growth

The entire UI must remain visually consistent across all pages.
